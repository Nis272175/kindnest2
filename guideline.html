Donation Guidelines

Learn about our donation standards and requirements to ensure your contributions make the maximum impact.

✨

Quality Standards

All donated items must be clean, functional, and in good condition. Items should have at least 6 months of usable life remaining.

📦

Packaging Requirements

Items should be properly packaged to prevent damage during transport. Use appropriate boxes and padding materials.

📅

Scheduling

Schedule your donation drop-off at least 24 hours in advance. This helps us prepare adequate space and staff.

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our donation process.

What items are not accepted? +

We cannot accept:
- Broken or damaged items
- Items with missing parts
- Expired products
- Hazardous materials
- Used personal care items

How do I prepare items for donation? +

To prepare your items:
1. Clean all items thoroughly
2. Check for completeness
3. Package fragile items carefully
4. Label boxes with contents
5. Keep related items together

What documentation do I need? +

Please bring:
- Valid ID
- List of donated items
- Any warranty cards or manuals (if applicable)
- Original packaging (when possible)
