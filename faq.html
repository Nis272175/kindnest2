Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating and receiving items through KindNests

Getting Started

How do I start donating items? +

To start donating, simply create an account on KindNests and click the "Start Donating" button. You'll be able to browse organizations in need, list your items, and arrange delivery or pickup.

What types of items can I donate? +

We accept a wide range of items including clothing, furniture, electronics, books, and household goods. All items should be in good, usable condition. Check our donation guidelines for specific requirements.

Donation Process

How does pickup/delivery work? +

You can choose between arranging your own delivery or scheduling a pickup. For pickups, you'll select a convenient time slot, and our partner organizations will coordinate the collection of items.

Can I get a tax receipt for my donation? +

Yes! After your donation is received, the organization will provide you with a tax receipt through our platform. You can access all your donation receipts in your account dashboard.

Registration & Setup

How do we register our organization? +

To register, click "Register Organization" and provide your organization's details, including your 501(c)(3) documentation. Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days.

What are the requirements for organizations? +

Organizations must be registered nonprofits, have a physical location for receiving donations, and maintain regular hours for donation processing. You'll need to provide proof of nonprofit status and insurance documentation.

Managing Donations

How do we list our needs? +

Once registered, you can create a needs list through your dashboard. Specify items needed, quantities, conditions accepted, and any special requirements. You can update this list at any time.

How do we coordinate pickups? +

You can manage pickup schedules through your dashboard. Set available time slots, assign volunteers or staff for pickups, and communicate with donors directly through our platform.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is here to help. Contact us directly for assistance.
